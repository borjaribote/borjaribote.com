About me

I am Borja Ribote, born on 21/12/1990 (28 years old) and live in London since 12/10/17 (1 year).

My decision to come to London was not because of work, or because of the crisis in my country. In fact, I left my job as a developer, in a big company, to come to London. So my decision to travel and live in another city away from everyone, was not driven by necessity, but by illusion.

My personality is based on humour, I consider myself a happy and positive person. Working I try to transmit good energy to others, I like to help and encourage people, as I consider it fundamental for a person. So when it comes to teamwork, I think it's very important to stay together, because that way, we would support each other, we would encourage each other and we would achieve bigger goals.

Academically, I haven't achieved the best results, and I haven't studied any university degree, but that's because my passion and decision to be a developer came late and abruptly. Totally changing my life, I decided to study something I didn't know, but I was intrigued and that's where I discovered my vocation. It was very difficult for me to pass the course, because unlike the others I had no previous knowledge, but the effort is something that differentiates me from others and thanks to that, not only did I pass the course, but I entered a company above people who had priori more knowledge than me.
I have a lot to learn, and that is something I love, as I enjoy learning a lot and especially applying the knowledge. So I would love to continue training and grow as a web developer.

To finish I would like to end with a reflection, a phrase that accompanies me day by day and is responsible for you reading this right now. I'm a dreamer, someone who thinks he's capable and some see him as crazy, but the people who are crazy enough to think they can change the world are the ones who do. So, think different.

Thank you very much,

Borja Ribote

My last project

This portfolio is my last work, here, in my small space within the Internet, I show those ideas carried out, ideas transformed into hours of work and study, based on the illusion, curiosity and confidence to do a great job.

Seeking to obtain different results, I like to turn things around, going out of those rules that establish that something is in a way by the simple fact that everyone does it the same. That's why I don't like to leave anything to chance, every color, every shape, positioning etc. It has a meaning and an explanation, and that has led me to investigate and learn things about UX/UI, typography or color psychology.

One of the most recurrent sources has been the internet, online courses and websites have been of great help in carrying out these projects. Thanks to them I have acquired all my knowledge about Photoshop and JavaScriptand improved those of HTML and CSS mainly.

In order to optimize the page, it has been created in a simple, intuitive and adaptable way. Using flexbox to adapt the page to any type of screen, a correct web positioning for easy search, and a simple user interface.

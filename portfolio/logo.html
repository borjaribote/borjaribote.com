Company Logo

This logo was the most important project, as the logo was proposed by a company. To explain it better I have divided it in three blocks (icons, letters and colors) where in each one I explain his motive, and another block, where I explain the personal card made by the logo.

Icons: The company, dedicated to construction, expressed me the requirement to represent all the services they offered. What I decided was to center the name and surround it with icons that indicated each of the guilds, but as the services offered were only 4 I decided to complement them with icons that represented safety, perfection and magnitude in the field of construction.

Letters: The name of the company (birgaitze services group) seemed to me that it covered too much and that the only word with value was the last one, therefore, I decided that the name would increase until arriving at the most important word that is shown with a larger font size and stands out even more due to the blue color.

Colors: The blue color is due to the fact that it is a new company formed by experienced workers. Therefore, along with gray, I wanted to represent both the freshness and innovation of being a young company and the experience and respect of being very experienced workers.

Personal Card: Here is where everything connects, in the center we observe strong hands, with wrinkles and symptoms of a life of hard work, which hold, but at the same time offer the logo representing the company itself. In the background a battle of colors, where blue (freshness), is invaded by gray (experience) revealing the part that most characterizes them.

In conclusion, in the card they want to indicate us their offering of services of confidence, without traps, and with an open mentality to new possibilities. /p>

These projects were both made with Photoshop.

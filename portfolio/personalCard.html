Personal Card

Seeing the results obtained with the previous CV, the same person decided to complement it with a personal card to try to get a job as a personal teacher. That led me to make the card as if it were a blackboard, highlighting the most important aspects of the classes and giving it a childish and humorous touch with the intention of showing the age range to which it is directed.

The back side caused me a dilemma, since it was between leaving it blank with the intention of being used as a notepad or including a small game for the fun of the child. In the end, I opted for the second because I considered that the intention for the child to play with it could cause the card to become the center of attention and remain in view, instead of being directly discarded.

This card was a work done with Photoshop.
